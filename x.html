Delta Youth Empowerment Program

Delta Youth Empowerment Program (DYEP) is a program funded by the Government of Delta state
His EXCELLENCY, Sen. Will Ifeanyi Smith jnr.
to empower youth to make them useful to their society, nation and the world at large.
It has been setup to empower the youth with trending ICT SKILLS that will sound-proof their future
and make them globally relevant.

REQUIREMENTS TO BE A PARTICIPANT OF THIS PROGRAM

  • Must have an o'level certificate
  • Proficiency in Reading
  • Must not be in a job to avoid distractions
  • Have a laptop
  • Have basic computer knowledge
  • Must be an Indigene of the state
  • Must be 21yrs and above

  • To be a success story of this empowerment program, self belief, zeal (determination) and a 100% practical based mindset is needed.
